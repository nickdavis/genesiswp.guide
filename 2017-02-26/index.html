StudioPress is offering a free migration to their new StudioPress Sites platform but only through 27 Feb 2017 (tomorrow at the time of writing). Here’s their instructions on to do it:

  • Choose your [StudioPress Sites] plan and billing cycle to check out.
  • You’ll be guided through the site setup process.
  • On the screen that says your site is ready, click the ‘See Migration Options’ button.
  • Choose the Fantasktic migration option.
  • Create an account at Fantasktic and submit your site for migration.
  • Check your email for the payment link, then use the code MOVE for your free migration.
