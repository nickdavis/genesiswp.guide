This Week in Genesis (28 August 2015)

28 August 2015 by Nick Davis


Photo by goodfreephotos.com

So after a brief pause / summer holiday / minor bit of soul searching, I’m very pleased to be back with This Week in Genesis #15 and – like summer anywhere but the UK – it’s hot, hot, hot with new themes, beautiful sites in the showcase and some great tutorials from Bill Erickson.

Themes

First up a beautiful new theme from Lauren Gaige over at Restored 316 by the name of Faithful.

Introducing Faithful: An Ecommerce Photography #genesiswp theme! http://t.co/b52JkPYNX7 pic.twitter.com/Jw6QEcxpUD

— Lauren Gaige (@restored316) August 26, 2015

Community legend, Bradley Potter, shares Amazing Times, an attractive (and free!) Genesis theme. Download via GitHub.

Many months ago I created a free #genesiswp news/magazine theme https://t.co/b4It7iTXCf

— Bradley Potter (@bradleypotter) August 21, 2015

Showcase

Ryan Townley with a well executed site for nextbyathena.com

Just launched!! Brand spanking new #genesiswp site for NEXT Active & Swimwear… http://t.co/D4dyK1wyge pic.twitter.com/vFrf82pnQY

— Ryan Townley (@ryantownley) August 26, 2015

Nice new site by Helena Denley, the wpwebsitecoach.com

New website, new brand – built on #WordPress and #GenesisWP – excited about a fresh new look – http://t.co/7KUoR7lew7 pic.twitter.com/9a13hVo7MI

— Helena Denley (@helenadenley) August 26, 2015

New site by Frank Herr at Best Light for bcbsdharma.org

Our new #responsive design for Barre Center for Buddhist Studies is live! https://t.co/axAcajfc4q #genesiswp pic.twitter.com/okaTfZecty

— best-light.com (@bestdashlight) August 27, 2015

And finally a clean new design for mutant.com.sg from Shaan Nicol.

Podcasts

The fine folks over at the UK Genesis Podcast are back and this week they spoke to Craig Simpson a web developer from from Fraserburgh in Scotland.

We're live! http://t.co/gI7Idrx0ay pic.twitter.com/R6UDR6WKtU

— UK Genesis (@UKGenesis) August 26, 2015

Meanwhile over on the OfficeHours.fm podcast, Carrie Dils caught up with WordPress core contributor, Mario Peshev.

I’m LIVE with @no_fear_inc! Tag your questions with #officefm http://t.co/oYMeLobCcl

Articles

Some great new tutorials from Bill Erickson, Display menu items with equal widths and Using Site Icons with Genesis.

Have an awesome weekend.
