This Week in Genesis (15 Jul 2016)

15 July 2016 by Nick Davis


Photo via Pexels

This Week in Genesis my thoughts are those affected by the attack in Nice, but I wrote 90% of this week’s edition yesterday (Thursday) so it seemed silly not to share it with you all still. And this week there’s:

  • New themes,
  • Rainmaker news,
  • A new podcast,
  • Plus the usual round-up of fantastic tutorials and showcase sites from the community.

As ever, I hope you find it useful and if I missed anything, free free to share it with me via the homepage.
