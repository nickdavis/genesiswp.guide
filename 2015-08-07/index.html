The best new WordPress Genesis things

This Week in Genesis (7 August 2015)

7 August 2015 by Nick Davis


Photo by Minimography

Hello and a very warm welcome to This Week in Genesis #14.

August is here which means holidays for many, but there’s no let up in things happening in Genesis with some beautiful new Genesis based sites in the showcase, theme teases and a Genesis 2.2 release date update.

Plus, your chance to be a guest editor of This Week in Genesis and see your name in lights (or pixels). Details at the end of this week’s article.

Genesis 2.2 release date update

First this week, an update from Genesis lead developer, Nathan Rice, that Genesis 2.2 is looking like it will be released on Monday 10 August.

If you're waiting for #GenesisWP 2.2 to release, it will most likely happen this coming Monday. Just FYI.

— Nathan Rice (@nathanrice) August 6, 2015

Themes

A sneak peek at a possible new real estate theme(?) from Brian Gardner.

As always, having a little fun with design —> http://t.co/tMSuLPOxn9 #genesiswp

— Brian Gardner (@bgardner) August 5, 2015

Confirmation that Davinder and Chinmoy over at Simple Pro Themes are working on their second Genesis theme, a few months after the release of Flex Pro.

Excited… the NEXT BEST Genesis theme for Business & Services websites is coming soon – #genesiswp goodness for everyone. @SimpleProThemes

— Davinder Singh (@idavinder) July 31, 2015

Showcase

First up one of the best new Genesis site designs I think I’ve seen in a while from Emily White (dev work by Greg Young) (h/t to Brian Gardner) on deliciouslyorganic.net

Excited to launch a brand new site design for http://t.co/leoBFIcHfx. @hbgreg did an amazing job on the dev of this custom #genesiswp theme.

— Emily White (@emwhitedesigns) August 3, 2015

Next a beautiful site design for Natalie Bourn. If you recognise the name that’s because it’s Jennifer Bourn and Brian Bourn’s daughter (and yes, you can guess who made the site…) nataliebourn.com

With a fresh new logo & self hosted #genesiswp site design, @NatalieBourn now has a look to match her sparkle pic.twitter.com/HGmUwtMKeb

— Jennifer Bourn (@jenniferbourn) August 4, 2015

You probably used to know him as Calvin Makes, but now he’s most definitely Calvin Koepke and he’s here with a fantastic new site calvinkoepke.com

So, I wrote about updating my site (among other things) — http://t.co/BnYCdKGMLB #genesiswp pic.twitter.com/ZAwcvxvV7G

— Calvin Koepke (@cjkoepke) August 6, 2015

Nice looking new site by Patrick McGilvray for corpconsciousness.com

Stunning new site just launched today! http://t.co/9Jt8LTzUNL #GenesisWP #WordPress @StudioPress @CobaltApps

— Patrick McGilvray (@patrick_mcg) August 6, 2015

Podcasts

In this week’s OfficeHours.fm, Carrie Dils speaks with Amanda Rush in a fascinating discussion about how Amanda, a blind developer, works with WordPress.

Curious to see how a blind WordPress developer works code magic? Join me tomorrow at 2pE with @cswordpress!

— Office Hours Podcast (@officehoursfm) August 5, 2015

Best of the rest

First up, a great Genesis feature round-up by Kristie Hill.

Not sure if your getting the most out of your Genesis theme? Check our these awesome features http://t.co/a0VFOlsqHK #genesiswp

— Kristie Hill (@blogambitions) August 3, 2015

Respected developer Tonya is now offering mentoring.

Want me to mentor you in #WordPress & #GenesisWP Software Development? #DevApprentice @WP_Devs_Club https://t.co/DMR7kZahu2

— Tonya (@hellofromTonya) August 5, 2015

A new plugin – Genesis Dambuster – integrates popular page builder Beaver Builder and Genesis.

Are you a #genesiswp and Beaver Builder user? The Genesis Dambuster plugin (free) makes full-width layouts a breeze: http://t.co/IgXmr3DJO5

— Beaver Builder (@BeaverBuilder) August 5, 2015

Oh and if you’re not already a member of the Genesis Facebook group, you really should be 🙂

Just read a thread on the #GenesisWP Facebook group, and wow. This community is absolutely second to none.

— Nathan Rice (@nathanrice) July 31, 2015

3300+ members of an unofficial, community run #GenesisWP Facebook group. That amazes me.

— Nathan Rice (@nathanrice) July 31, 2015

And finally…

I know we like to do giveaways from time to time over here on GenesisWP.guide, but this week there’s a great giveaway from Lauren Gaige over at Restored 316 Designs to win:

  • a cool planner,
  • any Restored 316 theme,
  • plus a copy of the Genesis Framework.

Competition closes on 12 August 2015.

That’s all for this week, apart from one special announcement / opportunity…
