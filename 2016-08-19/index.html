This Week in Genesis (19 Aug 2016)

19 August 2016 by Nick Davis

This Week in Genesis, food bloggers rejoice, a new foodie theme is here (and a new recipe plugin is coming soon); plus a deep dive into the world of Genesis starter themes; and not one, not two, but three new Genesis podcasts just for you.

Let’s get to it!

—

Themes

The fine folk over at Feast Design (the same peeps behind the Foodie Pro and Brunch Pro themes) have got a new theme out this week, Cook’d Pro. And, as you might expect, it looks pretty sweet!

The new Cook'd Pro theme by @feastdesignco is simply delicious — https://t.co/ckViIDlS70 #genesiswp pic.twitter.com/5CGQLjjauG

— StudioPress (@studiopress) August 16, 2016

In related news, Feast have also teamed up with the good people over at WP Site Care to launch a new recipe plugin, Cookbook. It’s not out yet but is available for pre-order (could be a good one for all your foodie clients…).

Start and grow your blog with a recipe plugin that actually works — https://t.co/Apyw0aajjb via @feastdesignco @wpsitecare

— StudioPress (@studiopress) August 17, 2016

And, elsewhere in theme news, Imagely (creator of many fine Genesis photography themes) have now launched their own lifetime all themes and plugins deal.
