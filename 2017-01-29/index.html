This Week in Genesis (29 Jan 2017)

29 January 2017 by Nick Davis


Photo via Unsplash

This Week in Genesis, Rainmaker Digital confirm they will launch StudioPress Sites in the next few days (and Brian Gardner answers a load of questions about it); new theme releases, plus the launch of Dynamik 2.0.

StudioPress Sites aiming to launch on Tue 31 Jan

Following on from last week’s announcement, Brian Gardner revealed this week that the new StudioPress Sites service from Rainmaker Digital is hoping to launch on Tuesday 31 January.

Billed as an alternative to SquareSpace, there’s been a lot of speculation about exactly what StudioPress Sites will and won’t be.

And while the only way to find out for sure is to purchase it at launch (they’ll be a 30 day money back guarantee if you decide it’s not for you), Brian has been popping up on the Gensis Slack and Genesis Facebook groups to answer a lot of questions too.

You’ll need to be a member of each group, but check out the following two threads for more information:

  • https://genesiswp.slack.com/archives/general/p1485466962001711
  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/genesiswp/permalink/1386970964687430/

Sridhar Katakam has also published a great round-up – StudioPress Sites is coming – What We Know So Far – over on his website, summarising it as:

a great move by StudioPress to put together the best of their Genesis themes, lessons from Synthesis and experience with Rainmaker service into one budget-friendly GenesisWP hosting offering competing with the likes of WP Engine and Pagely.

EDIT: There’s also a great round-up by Rebecca Gill over at Web Savvy Marketing (h/t Brian Gardner).
