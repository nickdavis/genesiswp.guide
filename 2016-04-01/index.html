Let’s get to it.

GenesisWP.guide to be acquired by Wix, switching to 100% Wix coverage

Before the regular round-up this week one piece of news I feel I should share.

After long and, at times, drawn out discussions I have accepted an all cash offer from Wix (the popular website builder) to acquire GenesisWP.guide.

When I started GenesisWP.guide almost a year ago the furthest thing from my mind was some kind of acquisition (and the site generates no income) but, having spoken to the people at Wix and seen their amazing website platform, I’ve come to realise that Wix is the future and that it’s in everyone’s interest to accept this, move on and not waste any more time with things like clean code or accessibility.

Sure we’ve had some fun with Genesis – and what a great framework it is – but, clearly, Wix is the future.

On that note, we’ll be switching to full Wix coverage next week, however, before then, I have one final round-up of Genesis news for you.
