This Week in Genesis (18 Dec 2016)

18 December 2016 by Nick Davis


Photo via Unsplash

It’s the last update this year (oh noe!), but I’ve got a bumper Christmas special for you packed with gorgeous new themes, handy tutorials, and some fine inspiration in the showcase.

Themes

Just when you start to think everyone put up their feet for Christmas already, along comes three (stellar) new themes.

First up, Pro Plus purchasers rejoice because you’ve got another new theme to play with over at StudioPress. Market is a third party theme from Restored 316, with out of the box styling support for WooCommerce, Genesis Simple Share, Simple Social Icons, Genesis Responsive Slider, Instagram Feed, and Genesis eNews Extended. Go grab it!

— StudioPress (@studiopress) December 14, 2016

Second, check out the fantastic looking Harmony Pro from Jamie Mitchell. Really dig this one.

And finally…

If I’m allowed to give a small plug to something I’m doing (after a year of plugging everyone else’s stuff 😉 ), I’ve started sharing links, notes and code snippets over at DesignToWebsite.com.

It’s been something I’m meaning to do for ages (and I mean a long time), but in a nutshell the plan is as I’m working on new project to just quickly add stuff to the site as I go.

There’s still a lot of improvements I want to make to it, but please do check it out and you can also subscribe for updates.
