This Week in Genesis (25 Mar 2016)

25 March 2016 by Nick Davis


Photo by Unsplash

This week my thoughts are with all of those affected by the attacks in Belgium.

However I still wanted to share the latest goings on from the one of the most awesome communities you’ll find in WordPress or anywhere else, that’s all of you in the Genesis community of course.

As always, thanks for stopping by, and wishing you and all your family a safe and happy Easter.
