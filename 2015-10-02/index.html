This Week in Genesis (2 Oct 2015)

2 October 2015 by Nick Davis

Photo by Minimography

Grab a coffee, because you’re going to need it to catch up with everything going on in This Week in Genesis #20!

Including two new eye-catching Genesis themes, some visual inspiration for you in the showcase, plus, tips on how to become a recommended Genesis developer.

Let’s get to it.

Themes

First up, the superstars over at StudioPress have launched a brand new theme, Workstation Pro, boasting a beautiful design (by Lauren Mancke) and accessibility support.

Invite Visitors to Your Creative World with Workstation Pro: http://t.co/RSwIgVQZmd #GenesisWP

— StudioPress (@studiopress) September 30, 2015

Next up, is a new, blog focused theme called Milton, from those lovely folk over at Lean Themes (disclaimer: I am one of the lovely co-founders at Lean Themes).

Milton has landed. Introducing our new blog theme for #genesiswp. Available now. http://t.co/Yp5HdaX4me pic.twitter.com/qixu6Nl87o

— Lean Themes (@Lean_Themes) October 2, 2015

Showcase

First up in the showcase this week, Shaan Nicol (of showcase legend) has an epic new site for sassyhongkong.com

Sassy Hong Kong – The girl's guide to everything Hong Kong Well folks, after a few months of development and help … http://t.co/4QXwwcJp9J

— ChillyBin Web Design (@chillybindesign) October 1, 2015

Next up, showcase regular Emily White (and developer partner in crime Greg Young) launching a great new site for happyhomefairy.com

Pleased to launch new custom #genesiswp design for http://t.co/0VOaTjjbdp. Huge thx to @hbgreg for an amazing job with the development.

— Emily White (@emwhitedesigns) October 1, 2015

The super talented Jessica Barnard launches a fantastic new site for thetravelbite.com

Just launched: A fun & colorful Foodie Pro theme customization for @TravelBlggr! http://t.co/nXmagX400Q #GenesisWP (cc: @shaybocks)

— Jessica Barnard (@thepixelista) September 25, 2015

And finally, a great new photography website from the Cre8tiveDiva for abbylynnphotography.com

I love doing photography websites. It was nice working with Abby. http://t.co/lGwrpOcXxd #genesiswp #wordpress #photographers

— Cre8tiveDiva (@TheCre8tiveDiva) September 27, 2015

For more Genesis showcase themes, check out the Genesis Showcase Facebook group.

Podcasts

No UK Genesis Podcast this week, but look out for their chat with WordPress co-founder Mike Little next week and send any questions you have for Mike over to Gary Jones.

If you could ask #WordPress co-founder @mikelittlezed1 any question, what would it be? #ukgenesis #genesiswp

— Gary Jones (@GaryJ) September 20, 2015

Over on the Office Hours Podcast, Carrie Dils is in the hotseat though and this week she’s chatting with Jackie D’Elia (who many of you in the Genesis community will know as @SavvyJackie).

Thanks for tuning in! You can find a replay of today’s episode with @SavvyJackie at https://t.co/hSi5tWZEVv #officefm

— Office Hours Podcast (@officehoursfm) October 1, 2015

Best of the rest

Genesis recommended developer and all round legend, Jesse Petersen shares his slides from his mobile-first talk at WordCamp Tampa.

As promised, my #wctpa Mobile-First Development slides are now up in SpeakerDeck at http://t.co/kGkFzmpHDf #genesiswp @wordcamptampa

— Jesse Ⓦ Petersen (@jpetersen) September 27, 2015

If you’ve ever wondered what it takes to be featured as a Genesis recommended developer, Brian Gardner has some tips for you.

@MelRichards_ A combination of helping in the forums, active on #genesiswp and overall in the community.

— Brian Gardner (@bgardner) September 25, 2015

An alternative to the Genesis Connect for WooCommerce plugin is released and developer, Aakdash Dodiya, is looking for feedback.

Connect Genesis Woocommerce is available in #wordpress now. https://t.co/rJtmxNlB02 give me some feedback folks. #genesiswp

— Aakash Dodiya (@DevelopersQ) September 25, 2015

The lovely Cre8tiveDiva launches a Genesis Signature Widget plugin.

New Genesis Signature Widget Plugin Adds Your Signature to Your Blog Posts! http://t.co/OW8mB8kCrU #bloggers #foodies #genesiswp #wordpress

— Cre8tiveDiva (@TheCre8tiveDiva) September 25, 2015

And finally, Genesis community legend Susan Ramsey has now retired Graphically Designing and is now One Happy Studio (and I’m very happy for her too!).

I'm happy to announce the "retirement" of Graphically Designing today. #GenesisWP – on to new things! http://t.co/tPSaweDzwF

— Susan Ramsey (@onehappystudio) September 30, 2015

And remember, for more WordPress and Genesis news every day, check out the Daily Bolt.

That’s all for this week, except to say, if you missed it last week you can now get more regular updates from GenesisWP.guide on Twitter (@GenesisWPguide).

Until next time, let’s keep Genesis awesome, and have an amazing weekend.
