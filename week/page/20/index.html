This Week in Genesis

A weekly round-up of all the finest and most exciting things going on in the world of Genesis WordPress including new themes, the latest articles and tutorials, and the best new websites created on Genesis.

This Week in Genesis (9 Oct 2015)

9 October 2015 by Nick Davis

WordPress co-founder Mike Little on the UK Genesis podcast; amazing new Genesis themes (and theme teases); plus, a whole lot of love in the showcase.

This Week in Genesis (2 Oct 2015)

2 October 2015 by Nick Davis

New themes from StudioPress and Lean Themes, tips on how to become a recommended Genesis developer, plus Jesse Petersen on mobile first development.

This Week in Genesis (25 Sep 2015)

25 September 2015 by Nick Davis

Theme bonanza! New themes from Minimal Genesis, Restored 316 Designs & WPStudio. Theme news from StudioPress. Plus news on accessible Genesis mobile menus.

This Week in Genesis (18 Sep 2015)

18 September 2015 by Nick Davis

Beautiful new theme teases, a new Genesis Showcase Facebook Group, and Pink & Press announces a cool competition, all in This Week in Genesis #17.

Genesis 2.2.2 released, new themes, and a new course from Carrie Dils, it’s This Week in Genesis #16.
