This Week in Genesis (27 May 2016)

27 May 2016 by Nick Davis


Photo by Barn Images

Hey all, This Week in Genesis there’s new themes,  a great new ‘all themes’ offer, and everyone’s talking about Blox…

Themes

I’m starting this week not with a new theme, but with a great offer from one of my favourite Genesis designer / developers, Ryan Townley.

Not only can you now grab all his themes over at Minimal Genesis for $99 (at the time of writing) but he’s including all his future themes too (and no, I’m not on commission, I just think it’s a great deal).

Save $60 on all themes now and get every future theme released: https://t.co/qEPkApPgQQ

— Minimal Genesis (@minimalgenesis) May 26, 2016

Back to our regular scheduled round-up then, this week sees another third party theme release on StudioPress, with Agent Focused Pro now available to buy (or download for free if you’re a Pro Plus member).

It’s a real estate theme by Winning Agent (and was coded by GenesisWP.guide favourite Marcy Diaz, who you should definitely follow if you don’t already).
