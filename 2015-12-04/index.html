This Week in Genesis (4 Dec 2015)

4 December 2015 by Nick Davis

Photo by Unsplash

This Week in Genesis (#29), a new Genesis community effort from Calvin, three awesome new premium themes and some event called WordCamp US?

Let’s go.

New thing from Calvin!

A new place for bloggers to post their work and gain exposure: Genesis Framework on Medium — https://t.co/mb7hRZuSvg #genesiswp

— Calvin Koepke (@cjkoepke) December 3, 2015

So, most of you know I feature a lot of stuff from Calvin Koepke on these updates, and deservedly so (the guy just puts out great stuff).

However this week I wanted to give a special shout out to something new he’s doing over on Medium.

But, I’ll let him explain it better than me 🙂

So, I’ve started a new idea: http://www.medium.com/genesis-framework/

I’m sure you get the idea by just looking, but the idea is to build the community up on Medium, and give people an avenue to publish their work and existing posts to a wider audience.

If you’ve got any value at all out of GenesisWP.guide, I definitely encourage you to check it out and support what he is doing over there too.
