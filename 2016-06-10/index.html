This Week in Genesis (10 Jun 2016)

10 June 2016 by Nick Davis

Photo by Unsplash

This Week in Genesis there’s so much going on I had to publish a little later than usual to fit it all in, but I can promise you the wait is worth it because there’s big Genesis news, gorgeous new themes, and enough tutorials to keep you occupied till next week’s edition.

—

Genesis 2.3 beta and translations update

Before we dive into themes this week, two bits of news.

First up, the Genesis 2.3 beta has now been released with a whole load of tweaks (check out the changelog on the previous following link).

If you’re a developer, grab the Genesis Beta Tester plugin now and have a play!

#GenesisWP 2.3 Beta Now Available.https://t.co/bXjERm8TdY

And hey, look! A changelog!

— Nathan Rice (@nathanrice) June 8, 2016

Next up, a big update on the future of Genesis Translations (ably managed by Remkus de Vries). I won’t repeat everything he says in his post but it sounds like a big improvement.

Huge thanks to Remkus for everything he’s done and keeps doing with Genesis Translations to all our benefit (and if you’re able to contribute on any of the translations, get involved!).

We just published an important update about the future of the Genesis Translations plugin here https://t.co/vkNwFUqANa #genesiswp

— ThemeMix (@ThemeMix) June 9, 2016
