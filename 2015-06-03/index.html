This Week in Genesis (3 July 2015)

3 July 2015 by Nick Davis

Well hello there and welcome to Week 9 of This Week in Genesis. This week you all absolutely killed it on new Genesis website releases, so it’s turned into a bit of a showcase special. In fact, there’s a whole lot of inspiration waiting for you below right now, so let’s get to it.
