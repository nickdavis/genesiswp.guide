This Week in Genesis (2 Oct 2016)

2 October 2016 by Nick Davis

Photo by Gratisography

Yes, it’s a Sunday edition again this week, but whatever the day, This Week in Genesis there’s so much going on you might just need a second cup of coffee before you finish reading it.

So what’s happening?

Well, there’s the release of Genesis 2.4; three gorgeous theme teases; plus Brian Gardner chatting to the Genesis dream team that is Brian and Jennifer Bourn over at Bourn Creative.

—

Genesis 2.4

It seems like only yesterday Genesis 2.3 was here but – thanks to lot of great work from Gary Jones and Ron Rennick especially – Genesis 2.4 (actually, Genesis 2.4.1 thanks to a couple of minor fixes) is already out in the wild.

Genesis 2.4 for WordPress Now Available — https://t.co/2hth6DxPaX #genesiswp

— StudioPress (@studiopress) September 28, 2016

I won’t repeat the entire release blog post but this version includes improvements to the Markup API; improvements to inline documentation and code standards; plus, a whole host of smaller enhancements and bug fixes.

#GenesisWP v2.4 for #WordPress Now Available https://t.co/eiQTMTAEDa pic.twitter.com/SCl96eXTC7

— Chuck Reynolds ? (@ChuckReynolds) September 28, 2016

And finally on the subject of Genesis 2.4, Gary Jones with your Genesis fact of the day…

Genesis 2.4.0 is the first major release to have fewer lines of comments than the previous release #genesiswp https://t.co/XXxzQpaRbb pic.twitter.com/jGBMl3dJli

— Gary Jones (@GaryJ) September 29, 2016
