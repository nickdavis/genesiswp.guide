This Week in Genesis (6 Nov 2015)

6 November 2015 by Nick Davis


Photo by Minimography

This Week in Genesis (#25) there’s no one ‘big’ Genesis story, just a whole lot of awesome, including: a free theme, some extremely good looking theme teases, amazing showcase work, and some fine tutorials.

In fact it’s one of our most packed weeks ever.
