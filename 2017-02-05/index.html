This Week in Genesis (5 Feb 2017)

5 February 2017 by Nick Davis


Photo via Unsplash

This Week in Genesis, StudioPress Sites goes live; donate to ACLU and get a free Design Palette Pro basic licence; plus, a free, new course for WordPress beginners to level up their PHP development skills.

StudioPress Sites launches

After lots of speculation, StudioPress Sites went live this week over at studiopress.com, where there are a lot more details and a handy FAQ.

If you’re wondering what it looks like behind the scenes though you might want to take a look at these two video walkthroughs from Erin Flynn and Kristie Hill. Edit (6 Feb): Helena Denley has also published a video walkthrough.

Not interested in Sites? Don’t worry all of the previous StudioPress themes are still available to purchase (and use on your own hosting plan), via the Shop For Themes page.

Brian Gardner’s also been continuing to answer questions over on the Facebook Group.

Of course it’s too early to say exactly how StudioPress Sites will turn out, but Rainmaker Digital’s Brian Clark is clearly happy with the reaction and what his team have done.

In fact, the @studiopress and #genesiswp crowd is owning it. Thank you, and more goodness is coming your way.

— Brian Clark (@brianclark) February 2, 2017
