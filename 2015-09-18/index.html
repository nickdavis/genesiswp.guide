This Week in Genesis (18 Sep 2015)

18 September 2015 by Nick Davis

Photo by Minimography

Well hello there Genesis people. It’s Friday which round here means it’s This Week in Genesis (#18 if you’re counting).

And this week I have teases of some beautiful new themes, a new Genesis Showcase group on Facebook, Pink & Press announcing an awesome competition, plus I reveal who this Monday’s exciting interview guest is on GenesisWP.guide.

Let’s get to it.

Themes

Another theme tease this week from Brian Gardner for his Atmosphere Pro theme. Coming along nicely…

What do you think of this design? http://t.co/DFQ04FZdh3 #genesiswp

— Brian Gardner (@bgardner) September 17, 2015

And speaking of theme teases, Lean Themes (disclosure: I’m the co-founder) teased a new Genesis theme, launching soon, Milton.

Coming soon for #genesiswp… Milton http://t.co/NEJWhSN3Ol pic.twitter.com/1VAi4NJecx

— Lean Themes (@Lean_Themes) September 18, 2015

Elsewhere, in update news, WP Canada announces they have updated all their themes to support the new Genesis 2.2 accessibility features.

All of our child themes have been updated. BlogNews retired. #genesiswp #WordPress http://t.co/DWYLmVrMGv

— WPCanada (@wpcanada) September 14, 2015

Plus, Web Savvy Marketing announces they are updating all of their themes to support WPML (a popular WordPress multi-language plugin).

WPML Compatible WordPress Themes Have Arrived! http://t.co/2z5eK7wSax #wordpress

— Web Savvy Marketing (@WebSavvyMrkting) September 16, 2015

Showcase

First up this week is this beautiful wine website by Carleigh Rochon (based on the Flex Pro theme) for brashhiggins.com

http://t.co/W5TGV0Cvgp – Cool website on Flex Pro theme. Creative header and footer implementation #genesiswp #WordPress

— Simple Pro Themes (@SimpleProThemes) September 18, 2015

Thauna has a beautiful new site for glutenfreebaking.com

Recently launched http://t.co/fkZejdBEW4 – made with love & #genesiswp – find something new & yummy to bake! pic.twitter.com/KGrJVLinq5

— Thauna (@BellanoStudio) September 16, 2015

And if the previous Flex Pro based theme wasn’t enough, here’s another great site based on it for notesofnomads.com

http://t.co/qpJ2yJKvLg – stumbled on this website using Flex pro theme. Nice subtle customization, specially creative logo image. #genesiswp

— Simple Pro Themes (@SimpleProThemes) September 15, 2015

Plus, I hadn’t seen it before but if you just can’t get enough new Genesis showcase designs, check out the recently launched Genesis Showcase group on Facebook.

Podcasts

On OfficeHours.fm, Carrie Dils talks to new WP Developers Club CTO and Genesis Developer Dave Grohl Alain Schlesser.

I’m LIVE with @schlessera! http://t.co/De93NFadPB #officefm

— Office Hours Podcast (@officehoursfm) September 17, 2015

Best of the rest

Wes Linda shares his Getting Started with Genesis slides from WordCamp Baltimore.

Hey @wordcampbalt groupies! Here are my slides from my talk on Getting Started with Genesis! https://t.co/qt6o0B9BCB #wcbalt #genesiswp

— Wes Linda (@WesLinda) September 12, 2015

And finally…

Last week I brought you the exiting news of the launch of new feminine WordPress theme marketplace, Pink & Press.

And this week you get the chance to win one of their beautiful themes plus an Emily Ley Simplified Planner in their competition.

Plus, if that wasn’t enough, I’ll also have an interview with Restored 316 and Pink & Press founder Lauren Gaige on Monday and your chance to win a Restored 316 theme.

—

That’s all for this week. I’ll be back on Monday with the Lauren Gaige interview and competition.

Until then, have an awesome weekend and subscribe!

PS Did I miss something awesome this week? Give it a shout out in the comments below.
