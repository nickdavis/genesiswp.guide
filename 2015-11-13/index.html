This Week in Genesis (13 Nov 2015)

13 November 2015 by Nick Davis

Photo by Gratisography

This Week in Genesis (#26) Calvin Koepke makes an epic comeback (with free themes + more), a great Genesis ‘starter talk’ by Sal Ferrarello, plus a community theme explosion for StudioPress Pro Pack customers.

Cominciamo…

Themes

First up, don’t call it a comeback, but Calvin Koepke is most definitely back and he’s rocking out free themes and other goodness (more on that later) all over the place.

In fact, if you go ahead and register at his site now, you can grab two free themes (Minny+ and Concise), now re-released in all their glory.

I've made a few themes available to download for subscribers. It's free! — https://t.co/a6h7LbN6yz #genesiswp

— Calvin Koepke (@cjkoepke) November 12, 2015

Next up, Lauren Gaige has released a new minimal Genesis e-commerce theme, Dainty.

Introducing Dainty: designed & built to spotlight your work with minimal distractions … https://t.co/1vh4dDqfKk #genesiswp #wordpress

— Lauren Gaige (@restored316) November 11, 2015

A first Genesis premium theme launch from Jonathan Subacz (SMG Themes), Day Four.

Introducing Day Four theme #genesiswp https://t.co/CVv9uV6k1z via @discoversmg

— SMG Themes (@DiscoverSMG) November 12, 2015

Next up, I keep giving WPStudio some love but I think they deserve it  with the latest peek of their forthcoming Forest theme and it’s looking good.

Another sneak peak of my upcoming Genesis Theme. https://t.co/xNWs1jU4SH #GenesisWP #Wordpress #WooCommerce

— WPStudio (@wpstudiowp) November 12, 2015

(And if you’re smart enough to post a weekly Twitter update of what you’re working on with a nice large thumbnail image that’s useful for someone that writes a weekly update full of embedded tweets, maybe you deserve a bit of love… 😉 ).

Podcasts

No UK Genesis podcast this week, but over on OfficeHours.fm, Carrie Dils has an awesome chat with Robby McCullough of Beaver Builder, a popular page builder plugin that’s no stranger to a mention on this very website.

We’re LIVE! Ask questions by tagging your tweet with #officefm https://t.co/MOtQ0X6eDq

— Office Hours Podcast (@officehoursfm) November 12, 2015

Oh and next week Carrie will have some guy called Nick Davis on the show apparently? Not sure who that is, I’d probably give that a miss. Yup, definitely not worth tuning in.

Best of the rest (or what Calvin Koepke has been up to this week)

Yup, not only has Calvin Koepke taken over Twitter this week, he’s taking over this update as well, but deservedly so, with a behind the scenes look at his relaunch (+ what’s next), tutorials and snippets too.

A Behind-the-Scenes Look at My Relaunch, And What‘s Next — https://t.co/Rk8ka5y0rQ #genesiswp pic.twitter.com/HKgZguzKL4

Complex and in-depth tutorials, made just for you. Because I love you. https://t.co/RtVZSDdhmV #genesiswp

An archive of Genesis Framework snippets. More coming — https://t.co/5rmA6sVMPL #genesiswp

Last week I featured slides from a couple of presentations that went down at the Phily ‘burbs WordPress meetup, now I’m happy to be able to share a video of Sal Ferrarello’s talk ‘Hooked on Genesis’ too, with a great explanation of how Genesis was the key to Sal’s understanding of WordPress as a whole.

Rafal Tomal shares a great article on the making of his Journal theme.

Making of: Journal Theme https://t.co/jRN1ffXAuA

If you’ve been admiring the social media icons in Brian Gardner’s new site redesign, here’s a tutorial on how to display social media links with Ionicons.

Just published a new tutorial — https://t.co/wdp4widi41 #genesiswp

— Brian Gardner (@bgardner) November 9, 2015

ConvertKit (which half the marketers I know seem to be getting very excited about) announces that it now integrates with the popular Genesis eNews Widget plugin (h/t Gary Jones).

4 new @ConvertKit integrations: ClickFunnels, Zapier, SendOwl, and the Genesis eNews Widget — https://t.co/7yoBwKzJqB

— Nathan Barry (@nathanbarry) November 6, 2015

Oh and Rebecca Gill over at Web Savvy Marketing is doing a 25% off sale…

Don’t Wait for Black Friday and Cyber Monday, Grab Your Savings Today! https://t.co/0dtkUMfn3N #wordpress #genesiswp

— Rebecca Gill (@rebeccagill) November 12, 2015

And finally…

Speaking of sales, the folks over at Copyblogger (the folks behind StudioPress and the Genesis Framework) are doing a sale on their Pro Pack which gives access to all their StudioPress themes (there’s a lot) for a one time fee.

But this time it’s slightly different as for the first time they’re including community themes (like the smash hit Foodie Pro theme) as well.

It’s a theme bonanza, people.

Black Monday-to-Monday: Our Two Most Popular Deals, Back by Popular Demand https://t.co/vog1jSuBIp by @jerodmorris pic.twitter.com/FpGES9L68X

— Copyblogger (@copyblogger) November 9, 2015

…which also means a nice little bonus for existing Pro Pack purchasers (as Susan and Davinder point out) there are now free community themes in your StudioPress dashboard for you to download.

#prettycreative & #prettychic now included in the #GenesisWP Pro Pack. @Copyblogger @lindseyriel https://t.co/bbLiOuBo8g

— Susan Ramsey (@onehappystudio) November 10, 2015

FYI for #genesiswp Pro Pack subscribers – 3 community themes available on downloads page now: Foodie, Pretty Chic & Pretty Creative

— Davinder Singh (@idavinder) November 9, 2015

And that’s it.

Always a sad moment, but don’t worry, dear reader, This Week in Genesis will return very soon with another update to warm your heart and get the pulse racing again.

Until next time, stay awesome and feel free to use that big old comment box below…
