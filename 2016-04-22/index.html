This Week in Genesis (22 Apr 2016)

22 April 2016 by Tony Eppright


Photo by Scott Penner (Dove photo used on social media by Liz West)

I imagine there’s been a ton of websites designed and a ton of code developed while listening to music by Prince. This week we lost one of the great artists of all time…thank you for all that you’ve given us! RIP Prince.

Onto the latest #genesiswp news, with a great opportunity to talk to Brian Gardner (yes, that Brian Gardner), a new theme release, lots of great showcase work and some tutorials to help you along the way.

Hire Brian!

Without Brian Gardner there would be no #genesiswp and we wouldn’t have this vibrant community! Here’s your chance to show your appreciation, while at the same time helping yourself.

Brian is running a campaign to raise $5,000 for orphan care, and to support his good friend and founder of The Hope Effect, Joshua Becker.

Until June 1, 2016, you can donate $250 to his campaign. Besides helping a worthy cause, you get an hour of Brian’s time to pick his brain. I was able to donate and spend an hour talking with Brian on a previous campaign and it was such a great experience…I can’t recommend it enough.

You can visit Brian’s website to learn more and donate to a worthy cause:
